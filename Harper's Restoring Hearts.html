Harper's Restoring Hearts

Our mission is to assist veterans in the Paris, TN and Murray, KY areas. Through both religious and non-religious means, we provide support for veterans dealing with a variety of challenges. This includes helping with non-violent legal issues, aiding in recovery, and ensuring veterans can attend necessary VA appointments. Beyond direct assistance, we are dedicated to the education of veterans about benefits, available assistance, and more.
